Beef

Globally, and in common parlance, beef is taken to mean any bovine meat - Ox, bull, buffalo or cow. However in India, beef has a more narrow interpretation in the common mind - it means cow meat. Sushi

Sushi is made of small pieces of raw fish that are wrapped in rice and seaweed. The chefs use a type of vinegar that is made from fermented rice to flavor the rice that is used to surround the fish and spices. Finally, the roll is wrapped up with some of the nori.While many people assume that sushi is also raw fish, it is actually vinegar rice that is mixed with a number of other ingredients.
